Spray In Truck Bedliners

At Tuff Skin, we specialize in Custom Urethane Applications. We’ve been spraying truck beds (and other things) since 1994. We know that purchasing a new truck or other vehicle is a big investment and applying a spray-in bedliner is the best way to protect that investment!

We stand behind our product and our customer service, offering a lifetime warranty on all Tuff Skin work. We are focused on customer satisfaction and are successful because of our hands-on approach to our business. We offer a nationwide warranty and stand behind our promise to satisfy every customer. Tuff Skin currently has dealers Nationwide and will continue to expand our dealer Network!
